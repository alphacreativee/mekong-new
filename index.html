Selector

The Mekong Enterprise Funds have typically focused on investments in retail, consumer products, consumer services, F&B, healthcare services, education, biotechnology and agrotechnology.

Founder Profile

We aim to invest in well-managed companies, founded by strong teams of entrepreneurial co-founders.

Growth Stage

We aim to invest in companies that already maintain healthy unit economics and are ready to accelerate their rate of growth. At this stage, companies would typically focused on refining their CAPEX and expansion plans, building their management team and building out the necessary infrastructure, including digital infrastructure, to support significant growth.

Geography

Mekong Enterprise Funds have invested only in companies that are headquartered in Vietnam or maintain significant operations in Vietnam.

Renewable Agriculture

Mekong Capital is currently developing an additional investment focus in renewable agriculture for the Mekong region.
